Sir

(randomly generated)

Sir was thinking about Adam and Eve again. Adam was sinful with red toes and pretty fingers.

Sir walked over to the window and reflected on his scary surroundings. He had always loved Stuyvesant with its mighty, moaning mountains. It was a place that encouraged his tendency to feel active.

Then he saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the intelligent figure of Adam and Eve.

The hail pounded like chatting rabbits, making Sir angry. Sir grabbed a giant blade that had been strewn nearby; he massaged it with his fingers.

As Sir stepped outside and Adam came closer, he could see the damp glint in his eye.

"I am here because I want an apple," Adam bellowed, in a witty tone. He slammed his fist against Sir's chest, with the force of 9589 bears. "I frigging love you, Sir ."

Sir looked back, even more angry and still fingering the giant blade. "Adam, I love you," he replied.

They looked at each other with shocked feelings, like two knobby, kindly koalas plotting at a very mean bar mitzvah, which had Taylor Swift music playing in the background and two hopeful uncles partying to the beat.

Sir studied Adam's red toes and pretty fingers. Eventually, he took a deep breath. "I'm sorry, but I can't give you an apple," he explained, in pitying tones.

Adam looked delighted, his body raw like a terrible, talented torch.

Sir could actually hear Adam's body shatter into 5449 pieces. Then he hurried away into the distance.

Not even a glass of brandy would calm Sir's nerves tonight.
